# Zentri Mission Architecture

## Three-Layer Structure (Required)
1. **Learn Layer**: Interactive exploration (min. 3 steps) where the student discovers the principle through play [18].
2. **Solve Layer**: Physical/Visual challenge. No MCQs. (e.g., "Hit the target where sin θ = 0.5") [18].
3. **Reflect Layer**: Reinforce the specific concept mastered (e.g., "You've locked sin 45° = √2/2") [18].

## Mission Catalog Highlights
- **Chapter 8 (Trigonometry)**: 7 Missions including "Triangle Anatomy," "Ratio Workshop," and "Identity Forge" [19].
- **Chapter 9 (Applications)**: 6 Missions including "Lighthouse," "Sun's Shadow," and "River Width" [20].
