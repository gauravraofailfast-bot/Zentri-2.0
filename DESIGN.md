# Zentri Visual Language

## Cinematic Dusk Aesthetic
- **Core Metaphor**: Deep indigo night sky fading into a warm ember horizon [6, 14].
- **Palette**:
  - `--sky-top`: #0a1028 (Deep Indigo)
  - `--ember`: #ff7849 (Action/Primary)
  - `--teal`: #5eead4 (Calm/Secondary)
  - `--sun`: #ffd166 (Special Angles) [14]
- **Glass-morphism**: Use `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)` and `background: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)` for all UI cards [14].

## Typography & UI
- **Headings**: Space Grotesk (Premium, game-like) [14].
- **Math/Numbers**: JetBrains Mono (Technical precision) [14].
- **Mascot**: Abstract glowing orb (No human representation) [14].
- **Interaction**: Touch-first mechanics (no mouse-dependency); use haptic feedback signals [15].
